# Cajun Seasoning

### Ingredients
- 3 teaspoons paprika
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoons garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Mix everything together.
